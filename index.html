Text to Speech (TTS) for Jejueo

제주어 음성합성 - Glow-TTS & HiFi-GAN 기반

This project is Single Speaker Text to Speech (TTS) for Jejueo. Glow-TTS(Generative Flow for Text-to-Speech is used for Text2Mel (Text to MelSpectrogram). HiFi-GAN(Generative Adversarial Networks for efficient and High Fidelity speech synthesis) vocoder is used for Mel2Audio (MelSpectrogram to Audio). The models are trained with JSS (Jejueo Single Speaker) Speech Dataset.

Glow-TTS and HiFi-GAN models are based on 🐸Couqi TTS
